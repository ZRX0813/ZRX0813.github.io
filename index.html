Research

Focus on creeping landslides and regional inventory mapping from SAR / InSAR, using the MB-Net family of networks that work directly on wrapped interferograms—without insisting that unwrapping is always the first step.

MB-NetV3 is the companion web map: tiled vectors, GMBA extent, and IFFI open-data overlays for exploring slow-moving slopes at regional scale. See the full publication list on the Research page or open the MB-NetV3 map.
